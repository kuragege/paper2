Postinflammatory hyperpigmentation

Chemical peels increase epidermal turnover and decrease epidermal melanin. Grimes12 pretreated 5 patients with PIH with hydroquinone 4% before treatment with a series of 5 SA 20% to 30% peels every 2 weeks: 80% had [75% improvement, and 20% had 51% to 75% improvement in PIH.12 Joshi et al73 replicated these findings using a randomized split-face model, in which 10 subjects with PIH received SA 20% to 30% peels on half of the face, and no treatment on the other half.
